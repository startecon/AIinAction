Forecasting the Wind Power Production

Overview of the Wind Mill Farm

Cycles in the Wind and Weather Conditions

  • Wind and weather conditions cycle through time but time timespan can vary
  • There is micro cycling in macro cycling

The Wind Mill Observations in Farm

  • Our sample data in the solution has multiple wind mills and multiple measuring points for observations
  • The metrics are temperature, wind speed, global surface radiation and wind power

Dynamics between the Observations Measuring Points

  • Observations from the earlier time points will be included to data row and the vectors
  • As combining historical observation time points to current observation point then dynamics between the observation points can be "memorized" to the data model

Flattening Multidimensional Data to Vectors

  • Current observation is combined to each sibling observation point from every location and altitude
  • Then all the data is flattened to one data row and data is normalized and tranformed to vector

Querying the Data Model

Vector Search

  • Query will search the most similar historical moment with the queried moment
  • Vector search looks the most similar vectors and returns best matches
  • Vectors A and B are vectors which are comapred in the picture

Cosine Similarity Search

  • Cosine similarity method calculates the correlation of two vectors
  • Vector search looks the most similar vectors and returns best matches
